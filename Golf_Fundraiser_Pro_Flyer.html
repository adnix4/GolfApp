Registration System

Three ways to register.
Every golfer covered.

Each mode is independently toggleable — close team slots when full while keeping individual registration open.

MODE 01

Full Team Registration

Captain registers the whole team in one session, or sends individual invite links to teammates to self-register into open slots.

Most Common
MODE 02

Join an Existing Team

Individual golfers register independently and find their team by name or via a captain's invite link. Entry fee split per-player or per-team.

Flexible
MODE 03

Free Agent — Need a Team

Solo players enter a Free Agent Pool. Organizer uses drag-and-drop pairing or one-tap auto-pair by handicap. Assignment email sent automatically.

Solo Friendly
